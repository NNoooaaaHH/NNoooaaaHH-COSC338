Similarities Between Compex and Simple Renders

Before I even had the basic understanding of computer graphics pipelining I have alsways been under the assumption that the graphicis pipeline was an extremely complicated process that would require many different steps and large amounds of code. I've always assumed the difference between rendering something simple such as a 2D polygon would be vasty different than rendering something complex like a 3D model or a fractal. However, after learning more about the graphics pipeline and familiarizing myself with the process of rendering, I have came to realize that the process of rendering something simple and something complex is actually very similar. Once you understand the core foundations behind the graphics pipeline, you will start to realize that the process is actually quite simple and is consistant across all types of renders.
